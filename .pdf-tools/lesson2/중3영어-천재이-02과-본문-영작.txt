Answers

1) For the science project,
2) our group has chosen very special insects.
3) They are very social.
4) They are as old as the T-Rex.
5) They appear in Aesop’s stories.
6) They use a special chemical to communicate.
7) Can you guess what they are?
8) Yes, the answer is ants.
9) We want to share with you
10) what we have learned about these insects.
11) How Many Ants Are on Earth?
12) We often see ants come out of nowhere.
13) Like humans, they live almost everywhere in the world,
14) except

a

few

extremely

cold

places

including
Antarctica.
15) As of 2018, there were over 7 billion people on Earth.
16) Then, how about ants?
17) According to scientists,
18) there are about one million ants
19) for every human in the world.
20) Though each ant hardly weighs anything,
21) one million ants are as heavy
22) as a human being weighing about 62 kilograms.
23) What Is the Ant Society Like?
24) Ants live in colonies which have lots of residents living
together.
25) Within a colony, there are usually three different types
of ants.
26) There is the queen,
27) and what she does her entire life is lay eggs.
28) The second type of ant is the male
29) that helps the queen produce these eggs.
30) The third type of ant is the worker.
31) Worker ants are all female and do very important jobs,
32) like

caring

for

eggs,

defending

the

colony,

and
collecting food.
33) How Do Ants Communicate?
34) Though ants do not speak like humans,
35) they actually have a “language.”
36) Ants produce a chemical called a pheromone
37) to communicate with one another.
38) By using the chemical,
39) they can exchange information about food or danger.
40) Ants also use touch for communication.
41) For example, if an ant finds food,
42) it passes on the good news
43) by rubbing its body on its neighbor.
44) Since an ant has legs covered with very sensitive hairs,
45) it can sense even the smallest touch.
46) FUN FACTS ABOUT ANTS
47) Some queen ants live up to 30 years.
48) Some ants can carry things
49) that are 50 times their own body weight.
50) Ants do not have lungs
51) but breathe through small holes in their bodies.
52) An ant has two stomachs.
53) One stomach holds food for itself,
54) and the other holds food to share with others.
55) Most ants can swim and live 24 hours underwater.
