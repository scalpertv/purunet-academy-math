## 초등 듣기 51~100회 완전 구성

### 제51회 듣기 — 주제: My Diary

**[Type A] 단어 듣고 그림 고르기 (5문항)**
yesterday / today / diary / morning / night

**[Type B] 문장 듣고 그림 고르기 (3문항)**
1. "Yesterday I went to the park with my mom."
2. "I wrote in my diary before going to sleep."
3. "Last Sunday was my birthday."

**[Type C] 짧은 대화 듣고 답하기 (2문항)**

대화 1:
> A: "What did you do yesterday?"
> B: "I stayed home and read a book."
> A: "Did you enjoy it?"
> B: "Yes, it was a really exciting story!"

Q: What did the boy do yesterday?
① Went to school ② Stayed home and read ③ Played outside ④ Watched TV

대화 2:
> A: "Do you write in a diary?"
> B: "Yes, every night before I sleep."
> A: "What do you write about?"
> B: "I write about what happened during the day."

Q: When does the girl write in her diary?
① In the morning ② After lunch ③ Before sleep ④ On weekends

**[Type D] 이야기 듣고 순서 배열 (1문항)**
이야기: 아침 일어남 → 학교 수업 → 친구와 놀기 → 일기 쓰기
그림 카드 4장 순서 배열

**[Type E] 받아쓰기 (2문항)**
1. "Yesterday was a great day."
2. "I wrote about my best friend."

### 제52회 듣기 — 주제: At the Library

**[Type A]** borrow / return / shelf / quiet / librarian

**[Type B]**
1. "Please be quiet in the library."
2. "I borrowed three books today."
3. "You must return this book by Friday."

**[Type C]**

대화 1:
> A: "Can I borrow this book?"
> B: "Sure. Do you have your library card?"
> A: "Yes, here it is."
> B: "Great. Please return it in two weeks."

Q: When should the student return the book?
① In one week ② In two weeks ③ On Friday ④ Next month

대화 2:
> A: "Where can I find books about dinosaurs?"
> B: "They're in the science section, on the third shelf."

Q: Where are the dinosaur books?
① Second shelf ② Fourth shelf ③ Third shelf ④ Near the entrance

**[Type D]** 도서관 이용 순서 배열 (입장→카드 확인→책 선택→빌리기)

**[Type E]**
1. "I love reading in the library."
2. "Books are my best friends."

**[지문 1] (5문장)**
> Monday was a great day. I went to school and learned about animals. After school, I played soccer with my friends. We won the game! At night, I wrote all about it in my diary.

R1: "diary"의 뜻은? ① 편지 ② 그림책 ③ 일기장 ④ 달력
R3: 방과 후에 무엇을 했나요? ① 책 읽기 ② 축구 ③ 수영 ④ 영화 보기

**[Part 4] 주제·어조 파악**

지문 (280단어):
> The debate between nature and nurture in behavioral genetics has undergone significant evolution. Early studies, often methodologically flawed by today's standards, sometimes overstated the role of genes. Modern researchers acknowledge that genes and environments are deeply intertwined. The concept of gene-environment correlation — where genetic tendencies influence the environments individuals select and are exposed to — complicates any simple separation of the two influences.
>
> Passive gene-environment correlation occurs when parents provide both genes and environment. Evocative correlation arises when a person's genetically influenced traits elicit reactions from others. Active correlation develops when individuals select environments compatible with their genetic predispositions.
